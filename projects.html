My Personal Website Reflecting on at Least 21 Years of My Living
(Summer 2021)


Description: Implemented Using HTML, CSS, and Javascript which reflects on everything I've experienced over at least the past 21 years ith my own eyes. This was implemented by me from May 18, 2021 to July 2, 2021 after self-learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript with a lot of time commitment (Total: 227 hours) and rarely any free time for me this half of summer

Takeaway: I think making my own website is probably the best idea that I can think off because my mind cannot retain all of my memories in one mindset. By keeping everything in my own website, it is my way of preventing all my memories from being lost in time. Also I found implementing my website to be fun because I have the freedom to add my own features to make it look interesting to web users. I think one of the most interesting part is when I made a mosaic of everyone I met throughout my life. One of the most challenging parts of making a website is making it look good for all devices since my website is responsive when used on different devices. Meaning some devices, my web appearance looks good but for the others, the appearance doesn't look good in terms of its formatting.

SPI Transmitter and Receiver (Spring 2020)


Description: Programmed in VHDL (VHSIC Hardware Description Language) - Simulated waveforms are generated in which each bit of data input from MSB is being transmitted to the SPI receiver to receive each bit of data from the SPI transmitter.


SCK is a serial clock that controls the shifting of data in the interfacing it is a clock signal.


SS_Bar (Slave Select Bar) is the control signal that signals to the slave device that data is ready to be transmitted in this case when SS_Bar goes from a logic 1 to logic 0.


POL (Clock Polarity) controls the polarity of the clock. Since SCK doesn't stays a constant value until its ready to transmit data CPOL determines that. If CPOL=1 clock stays at logic 1 when not transmitting data and the opposite when 0.


CPHA (clock phase) determines which edge data is shifted at relative to the clock pulses. If CPHA is a 1 a but is transferred at a leading edge of the clock and if CPHA is a a 0 its transferred at a trailing edge of clock.
