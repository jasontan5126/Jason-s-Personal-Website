2nd Post: Me as an Adult and My First In Person Event




As one week has passed by since my 21st birthday, life feels so different now that I am an adult in contrast to me as a child. In my experience, time has gone by pretty fast every year and there is no sign that it may slow as soon as I approach myself as an elder years from now.

However, being 21 has gone into a great start for me because I attended my first in person event ever since before COVID started. One of them was when I volunteered to help in preparation for the NYC triathlon after viewing it in the "CKI Gen-Bod 21-20" discord announcements. The reason I took advantage of this opportunity was to actually meet new people (hopefully at least one Stony Brook student) in person after spending time in remote for at least a year since COVID started.

To be honest, it was enjoyable and worth attending as I got to make some new friends that I've never met before. Most of them were from City College. Their names were Abdullah, Yussef, Kelly, and Jessin. We were together shaded a tent this whole day where we distributed accessories to participants for tomorrow's triathlon which was how I got to know them better. I especially got a chance to talk with Abdullah and Yussef a lot since we were together helping each other in the volunteer work. What we would talk about was our college experience, our daily life, and etc. Until suddenly, Kelly told me that there was a volunteer from SBU which turned out to be Jessin.

For some reason me and Jessin were pretty excited with meeting each other mainly because we hadn't met an SBU student in person for quite a while. We actually chilled out eating dinner while engaging in a nice conversation regarding life, school, etc. Somehow, she knew me because I attended the Francis Lewis Key Club Summer Social through Zoom. Anyhow I asked a question regarding her interest of being adding her to my mosaic. Somehow, she was pretty impressed with my idea of making a mosaic of my friends as part of web. Therefore, she expressed interest into being added by sending me a pic of herself through Discord.

After a long conversation with Jessin during dinner, our relationship grew unexpectingly especially when we talked continuously on our way home through subway late at night. I was even asked if someday during this upcoming fall semester, we can workout together in the SBU recreation center and I said "Yeah!" But I'll see what happens closer to this upcoming fall. As of now and most likely the future, I continue to keep in touch with Jessin through Discord whenever.

Looking back to my first week being 21, I think choosing to volunteer for the NYC triathlon was a good decision because I got a chance to make new friends in person in a while than through remote events. Although I wished I got to talk to the Key clubbers, they didn't look too interested into talking with Circle K'ers (college students). As I always say, it's never too late to continue the objective of making new friends or even developing a strong bond.
