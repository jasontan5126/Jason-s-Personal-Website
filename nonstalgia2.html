Cyberchase

🛈This is my favorite childhood show out of all the PBS Kids shows mainly because of the adventures the Cybersquad does to stop The Hacker from taking over Cyberspace. Also the For Real segment would air after the episode ended with Harry or Bianca starring the show. During my childhood I remember it airing at 4:30pm after I came back from elementary school because I was excited to see which episode would air. Good old days!




Mister Roger's Neighborhood

🛈I remember watching this as a kid on PBS Kids. This was probably the show that would help cure my depression when I was a child since I didn't had too many friends during that time. The opening for the music was what was catchy to making me watch this show since it was more relaxed. I find this better than the show: Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood in my opinion.

Bill Nye the Science Guy

🛈OMG! I remember this series whenever in science class at elementary school, my former teacher would pull up this show for our entire class to watch to educate us about science. The reason why I loved it was the idea that I wasn't just listening to plain old information to make me fall asleep, but I saw some drama and some cool experiments that would be performed by the main show host. I missed watching this show. Also the opening for each episode was pretty catchy.

Game Show (Watched as a child)




Wheel of Fortune

🛈This game show is the show where I've been watching for quite a while ever since childhood. It's like a game of hangman except you miss the wheel for cash by guess a consonant in the letter board for cash. You can read more about its rules in detail if interested. I remember using this show as part of my advantage to learn English as a child since a lot of words were unfamiliar to me. I'm still surprise that the two host are still hosting the show today even though I was expecting them to retire soon. But at least it's better to keep the game show tradition going. One of my favorite thing about this game show is actually seeing contestants fail at guessing the correct puzzles.
